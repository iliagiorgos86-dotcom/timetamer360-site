TimeTamer360 – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-09

Overview

TimeTamer360 is designed for privacy. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All usage statistics and analytics are computed locally on your device and remain there.

Data Collection & Processing

  • No account is required. We do not operate a cloud backend.
  • On‑device analytics only. Daily usage minutes, streak counters, and settings are stored in the app’s local storage/sandbox and never leave the device unless you back up the device yourself.
  • Deletion. You can delete all app data anytime via the Android system: Settings → Apps → TimeTamer360 → Storage → Clear data.

Permissions

  • Usage Access – Required to read foreground app usage time. Raw data stays on device.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) – Optional, for daily summaries and reminders.
  • Display Over Apps (optional) – To show a blocking overlay when limits are reached.
  • Receive Boot Completed – To re‑schedule background tasks after a reboot.

Advertising & Consent

The app may show optional, user‑initiated rewarded ads that allow you to earn extra screen‑time minutes. Ads never start automatically. We use Google Mobile Ads SDK and Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) for consent in applicable regions.

  • No personalized ads are shown without your consent.
  • If you do not grant consent, rewarded ads are disabled.
  • See Google’s privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children

The app is intended for general audiences and is not primarily directed to children.

Security

All data remains in the Android app sandbox. We do not receive or store any personal information on our servers.

Your Rights

  • You control the app’s permissions at any time in Android Settings.
  • You may clear all local data at any time via the system “Clear data” action.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@timetamer360.app.

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above and publish the new version at the same URL.
